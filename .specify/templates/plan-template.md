## Constitution Check

*GATE: Must pass before Phase 0 research. Re-check after Phase 1 design.*

- **Security-first plane separation**: Control-plane and enforcement-plane
  responsibilities are explicit; contract or sync changes are documented.
- **Privacy/GDPR**: No unencrypted PII, secrets, tokens, or tenant operational
  data are logged, cached, stored, exported, or exposed to clients.
- **Performance**: Normal user-facing API responses remain below 200 ms; any
  third-party latency is asynchronous, bounded, or surfaced as degraded state.
- **Quality gates**: PSR-12/Pint, PHPStan, ESLint for lintable JS/TS, Worker
  typecheck/build, and automated tests are identified for affected modules.
- **Coverage**: Plan preserves the >=80% coverage target or documents an
  approved exception with compensating verification.
- **UX consistency**: Operator/customer-facing changes include consistent labels,
  validation, loading, empty, success, error, and accessibility states.
- **Modularity and docs**: Modules, contracts, ownership, dependency boundaries,
  and documentation updates are identified.
- **Feature flags/trunk**: Risky or incomplete behavior is feature-flagged or
  otherwise configurable, with rollback/deactivation steps.
